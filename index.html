Hello! I'm Jon. I'm a writer living in Brooklyn. I hold an MFA in fiction from UNCW.

You can find my work in Hayden's Ferry Review, The Southeast Review, and Pleiades. In 2021, my poem "Blueshift" was an AWP Intro Journals winner.

I previously read for Ecotone magazine and taught undergraduate creative writing.

I play the saxophone, I love crossword puzzles, and I have a hound dog named Goose.
